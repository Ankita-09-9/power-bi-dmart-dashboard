🛒 DMart Sales Analytics Dashboard

📊 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive business intelligence solution built using Microsoft Power BI to analyze the sales performance of DMart.
The dashboard provides insights into sales trends, product performance, and customer behavior using visual analytics.
The report is divided into three analytical dashboards:
📈 Sales Dashboard
📦 Product Analysis Dashboard
👥 Customer Analytics Dashboard

These dashboards help in understanding business performance, identifying top-selling products, and analyzing customer purchasing behavior.

⚙️ Tools & Technologies
Microsoft Power BI
Data Visualization
Data Modeling
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

📈 1. DMart Sales Dashboard - 
The Sales Dashboard provides a high-level overview of overall sales performance and order trends.
Key Insights :-
Monthly sales trends and month-over-month growth are visualized.
Category-wise sales distribution shows contribution of Local, Branded, and Imported products.
Marketing channels like Instagram and Facebook drive a significant portion of sales.
State-wise sales distribution highlights geographic performance.

📦 2.DMart Product Analysis Dashboard
This dashboard focuses on product performance and evaluates how ratings and discounts influence sales.
Key Insights :-
Identification of Top 10 products by sales.
Analysis of Bottom 10 products with low performance.
Relationship between product ratings and sales.
Impact of discount percentages on sales performance.
Category-wise product performance analysis.

👥 3.DMart Customer Analytics Dashboard
This dashboard analyzes customer behavior and purchasing patterns.
Key Insights :-
Monthly order patterns of customers.
Customer distribution across age groups and gender.
Order distribution by product category.
Marketing channel influence on customer acquisition.
Customer payment preferences.

💡 Business Value:
This dashboard helps businesses:
Monitor sales performance in real time
Identify top-performing products
Understand customer demographics and behavior
Improve marketing strategies based on channel performance
Optimize product pricing and discount strategies
